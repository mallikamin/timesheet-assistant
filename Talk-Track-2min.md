# 2-Minute Talk Track (Client Call)

We reviewed your current process: calendar/manual effort feeds Harvest, and team leads approve at the end. The pain is not approval, it is the manual effort and weak audit quality from generic late entries.

Our Phase 1 solution is to auto-create Harvest draft entries using calendar signals plus browser activity context, then let users do a fast weekly review before submission. Team lead approval stays exactly as-is.

This gives you three benefits immediately:
1. Less end-of-week time logging burden.
2. Better audit trails in notes and activity tags.
3. Higher consistency in project/activity mapping.

For control and safety, we only auto-draft. Any low-confidence blocks go to a "Needs Review" queue instead of being guessed.

If we align today on mapping ownership, confidence threshold, and rollout mode, we can start implementation with an org-wide-ready Phase 1 design.
