1. Executive Summary

Sitara Infotech proposes an AI-powered timesheet assistant for Thrive PR + Communications that eliminates manual time entry by intelligently capturing work activity across Google Workspace and synchronising approved entries to Harvest.

The solution uses natural language processing and voice input to log time conversationally, scans Gmail and Google Calendar to suggest entries automatically, and introduces a draft-and-approve workflow — entries are reviewed by the user in a Google Sheet before anything reaches Harvest.

A fully functional proof of concept has already been built and demonstrated live. This document covers the production implementation for up to 60 users, priced to minimise recurring monthly cost while covering infrastructure, AI processing, and ongoing platform support.

3. Target Workflow

The production system operates on an approval-first model. No entry reaches Harvest without explicit user sign-off.

  1. Capture
    User dictates or types time entries, or the AI identifies activity from Gmail, Calendar, and Drive.
  2. Draft
    AI creates structured draft entries with project, task, hours, and notes. Low-confidence suggestions are flagged with clarifying questions.
  3. Stage
    Draft entries are stored in the app and mirrored to a Google Sheet — the user's personal timesheet register.
  4. Review & Approve
    User reviews entries, edits as needed, and approves. Managers can view draft and approval status across the team.
  5. Sync to Harvest
    Only approved entries are pushed to Harvest. Status updates from Draft → Submitted.
This workflow reduces trust risk at scale, creates a visible review layer for managers, and works regardless of which AI model powers the system.
